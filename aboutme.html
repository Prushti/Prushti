I'm an impact-driven designer who cares about designing efficient and enjoyable products for users.

Pursuing a career in UX Design was a natural transition when I realized that UX Design is right at the crossroads of my passions: to analyze how our brain perceives the information, delve into the reasons behind our behavior, approach problem-solving in a creative way and the eagerness to drive change.


The trigger was the moment when a designer shared his app idea and asked for my opinion and help to brainstorm how users would interact with the app. The more I inquired about the design process, I discovered that it is something in which I can use my research and analytical skills.

“We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit.” — Will Durant

I am very grateful for the years I studied Computer Science which help me to grasp new technologies easily. As a Software QA Engineer, my role was to test client/server based web applications which taught me how to identify bugs and test application with different perspectives. It helped me to update my skills constantly and implement strategies to overcome challenges in a highly collaborative environment.

My goal is to leverage my diverse experiences and UX Design skills to create products that are easy and enjoyable to use.

In my free time, I love gardening and cooking for my friends, lose track of time discussing ideas and will not miss the opportunity to travel. Have lived and studied in 3 countries, will eagerly learn a new language, I believe it brings people closer and makes any journey memorable.
